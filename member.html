Mr. JIANG, Shuzhou

PhD Candidate

Mr. Shuzhou Jiang received his master degree in Marine Technology from Norwegian University of Science and Technology in 2021. He completed his undergraduate education at Dalian University of Technology in 2019.

During his master education, he finished the thesis titled "Installation of Offshore Wind Turbine Blade Using a Semi-submersible Floating Installation Vessel" under the supervision of Prof. Zhen Gao.
